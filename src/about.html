Meet Our Team

The EventPipe Leadership Team

Tim Brown CEO

Tim is an experienced and accomplished software executive having served as the head of corporate development and as Chief Financial Officer at Motus, where he led the company through multiple financings and acquisitions. Tim joined Motus after serving as the Chief Financial Officer of ConnectEDU and leading the corporate development/M&A function at EducationDynamics. Tim began his career in investment banking and private equity after graduating from the University of Pennsylvania’s Wharton School of Business.

Mike Addesa Co-Founder, CTO

Mike is a seasoned executive with 25 years of experience developing and scaling high-tech software organizations. Prior to founding EventPipe, Mike was VP of Mobile Applications at Motus, scaling the mobile platform for the leading mobile workforce management software. His background also includes time as VP of Technology at RedNote and Principal Software Engineer at Fidelity.

Jeff Logan Vice President of Product

Jeff is a product leader with 10 years of experience in building, launching and scaling software products that deliver value. Prior to joining EventPipe, Jeff worked as Director of Product Management at Motus, and was a driving force behind their flagship mobile product. Jeff also spent time at Redfin as a Senior Product Manager. He has a degree in Economics from Boston College.

Angela Forte Senior Business Analyst

Angela is a diversified professional with over two decades of IT experience. Her background includes senior leadership roles in various industries including event management. She has deep experience not only in events, but in software implementation, integration and custom development. Angela is passionate about driving the alignment of IT standards and technologies in pursuit of achieving business objectives for EventPipe and each of its customers.

Patricia Driscoll Customer Success Specialist

Patricia is a proud Towson Tiger alumnus who jumped headfirst into the sport and hospitality market after graduating from Towson University in 2017. She has extensive experience in housing management, strategic negotiations, and event technology. Through these skills and a vast knowledge of the EventPipe platform, her passion lies in driving growth and revenue for the clients she manages.

Ben Lancaster Account Executive

Ben has been helping customers achieve success through a consultative sales effort for the past 11 years in events and technology services. Before he joined the EventPipe team, he grew up in the event industry and graduated with a Sports/Entertainment/Event Management degree, giving him the experience to truly understand the real-world problems our customers are facing today. He loves collaborating with his clients and coming up with solutions that help grow their businesses and events in a big way.

Josh Silverberg Product Manager

Josh is an accomplished Product Manager, having most recently led product efforts on the data warehouse team at Definitive Healthcare. Josh has a degree in Business from the University of Delaware.

Rob Warner Director, Engineering

Rob is a Software Engineer with 20 years of experience in leading and building engineering teams that deliver maintainable, extendable and feature rich solutions.
